## Leaderboard Teams

Leaderboard teams are an optional feature for leaderboards that allow you to assign players into teams.  Teams have their own rank in your leaderboard by the ranking method specified in your [leaderboard settings](https://www.construct.net/game-services/manuals/game-services/leaderboards/settings/teams).

Using the [get leaderboard teams](https://www.construct.net/game-services/manuals/game-services/leaderboards/api-end-points/teams/list-teams) API end point you can get a paginated list of all teams by rank order for your leaderboard.

You can create, rename and delete teams as well as assign players to teams, remove players from teams and delete teams using the [relevant API end points.](https://www.construct.net/game-services/manuals/game-services/leaderboards/api-end-points/teams/create-team)
